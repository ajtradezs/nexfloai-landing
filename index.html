FAQ

The questions
everyone asks first.

How much does it cost?
Most builds land between R50K and R80K once-off, depending on scope. We scope each engagement in the strategy call — no "starting from" pricing that means nothing.
How fast can you actually ship?
First working prototype in 5–7 days. Production deployment in 2–3 weeks for most builds. We ship in weekly slices, not quarterly Gantt charts.
What if I already have a tech stack?
Good. We integrate — we don't rip-and-replace. Our builds already sit on top of Google Workspace, HubSpot, Airtable, WhatsApp Business, Xero, custom SQL — whatever you've got.
What happens when something breaks?
Every system we deploy has monitoring baked in. We stay available for iteration and support after handover. Most clients keep us on a light monthly retainer; no one is required to.
Do you work with businesses outside South Africa?
Yes — we work async with clients globally. Most communication is over Slack, Loom, and scheduled calls.
Is this just ChatGPT in a trench coat?
No. We use Claude Code and the Anthropic API as build accelerators, but what we ship is production software: Python, Node, real APIs, real databases, real uptime. AI is how we build fast; it's not the product.
